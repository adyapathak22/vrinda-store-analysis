Vrinda Store — Annual Sales Analysis 2022
This is an end-to-end Excel data analysis project on Vrinda Store's 2022 sales data. I cleaned and preprocessed the raw data, built pivot tables, applied filters, and created charts to extract meaningful business insights across 6 key areas.

Total dataset: 31,047 orders | Total revenue: ₹2.11 Crore

What I analyzed
1. Sales vs Orders (Monthly Trend)
Tracked both revenue and order volume across all 12 months to spot peak and low seasons.

Peak month: March (₹19.28L, 2,819 orders)
Lowest month: November (₹16.15L, 2,383 orders)
Sales and orders follow the same trend — no major fulfilment issues
2. Men vs Women
Broke down total revenue by gender to understand the core customer base.

Women: ₹1.35 Crore (64% of revenue)
Men: ₹76.1 Lakh (36% of revenue)
Women are clearly the dominant customer segment
3. Order Status Breakdown
Analyzed delivery performance across all orders.

Delivered: 28,641 (92.2%) ✅
Returned: 1,045 (3.4%)
Cancelled: 844 (2.7%)
Refunded: 517 (1.7%)
Overall fulfillment rate is strong at 92%+
4. Top Sales Channels
Compared order share across all platforms.

Amazon dominates at 35.5%
Myntra second at 23.4%
Flipkart third at 21.6%
Meesho, Ajio, Nalli, and Others make up the rest
5. Top States by Revenue
Identified highest-performing geographic markets.

Maharashtra — ₹29.9L
Karnataka — ₹26.5L
Uttar Pradesh — ₹21L
Telangana — ₹17.1L
Tamil Nadu — ₹16.8L
6. Category & Demographics
Analyzed product categories, age groups, and size distribution to understand buying patterns across different customer segments.

Tools used
Microsoft Excel — data cleaning, preprocessing, pivot tables, charts, slicers, filters
Raw data had 31K+ rows across multiple columns (Order ID, Gender, Age, Channel, Category, Size, Amount, State, Status)

Key business insights
Focus marketing on women aged 20-50 — they drive the majority of sales
Amazon and Myntra together account for nearly 60% of all orders — prioritize these channels
Maharashtra and Karnataka are the top markets — good targets for regional campaigns
March is the best month — plan inventory and promotions around Q1
92%+ delivery rate is healthy, but the 3.4% return rate is worth investigating by category
